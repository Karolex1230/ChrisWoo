DJ ChrisWoo Event Policy

Booking & Payment

  • A 25% deposit is required to secure any booking. Full payment must be received at least 7 days before the event.
  • Payments are non-refundable except under cancellation by the client more than 30 days in advance.
  • The DJ reserves the right to cancel the booking if payment deadlines are not met.

Safety & Liability

  • The client is responsible for providing a safe environment for the DJ and equipment.
  • The DJ is not liable for personal injury, property damage, or theft occurring at the event unless caused by gross negligence.
  • The client is responsible for any damage caused to DJ equipment due to misuse or negligence.
  • The DJ reserves the right to stop performing if safety is compromised.

Event Environment & Requirements

  • Power, shelter, and suitable space must be provided for equipment setup, especially outdoors.
  • Noise restrictions imposed by local authorities or venue must be adhered to.
  • The DJ may refuse to perform in unsafe, illegal, or highly disruptive environments.

Cancellations & Force Majeure

  • Client cancellations less than 7 days prior to the event may result in full payment being retained.
  • DJ cancellations due to illness, travel restrictions, extreme weather, or other unforeseen events (force majeure) will include a full refund or rescheduling.
  • Rescheduling must be mutually agreed upon, and additional fees may apply if circumstances differ.

Music Requests & Conduct

  • Clients may submit song requests at least 5 days before the event.
  • The DJ reserves the right to refuse offensive, inappropriate, or non-compliant requests.
  • Clients and guests are expected to respect DJ instructions regarding equipment, stage, and performance area.

Media Usage

  • The DJ may take photos or videos of the performance for promotional purposes unless explicitly requested otherwise by the client.
  • Clients may request all media to be removed from promotional use prior to the event in writing.
